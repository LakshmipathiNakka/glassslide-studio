## Common Questions

### Q: Why can't I see the controls?
**A**: In fullscreen mode, controls auto-hide after 3 seconds. Just move your mouse to make them reappear.

### Q: Can I use fullscreen on a second monitor?
**A**: Yes! Move your browser window to the second monitor first, then start the presentation and press F.

### Q: Does fullscreen work on my iPad/iPhone?
**A**: Limited support. iOS Safari doesn't allow full document fullscreen, but the presentation will still work normally.

### Q: What's the difference between F and ESC?
**A**: 
- **F** â†’ Toggles fullscreen on/off, stays in presentation
- **ESC** â†’ Exits both fullscreen AND presentation, returns to editor

### Q: Can I customize the auto-hide time?
**A**: Currently set to 3 seconds by default. Future updates may allow customization.

### Q: Will my slides look different in fullscreen?
**A**: No! Content maintains the same aspect ratio and appearance, just larger to fill your screen.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Fullscreen doesn't activate
**Try these steps**:
1. Check if your browser supports fullscreen (most modern browsers do)
2. Look for a permission prompt in your address bar
3. Try pressing F instead of clicking the button
4. Refresh the page and try again

### Problem: Controls won't appear
**Solutions**:
1. Move your mouse more noticeably
2. Click anywhere on the slide
3. Press F to exit and re-enter fullscreen
4. Make sure you're actually in fullscreen mode (no browser UI visible)

### Problem: Screen looks zoomed or stretched
**Solutions**:
1. Check your display scaling settings (Windows: 100% recommended)
2. Reset browser zoom to 100% (Ctrl+0 or Cmd+0)
3. Try a different screen resolution
4. Exit and re-enter fullscreen

### Problem: Can't exit fullscreen
**Emergency exits**:
1. Press **ESC** key (always works)
2. Press **F** key to toggle
3. Click **Minimize** button if controls are visible
4. Press **Alt+F4** (Windows) or **Cmd+W** (Mac) to close browser tab
